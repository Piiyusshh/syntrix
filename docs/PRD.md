# Syntrix

## Product Vision

Syntrix is an AI-powered Business Intelligence Platform that helps organizations analyze business documents, sales reports, emails, and structured data using Machine Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

## Target Users

- Business Analysts
- Sales Managers
- Executives
- Operations Teams
- Small & Medium Businesses

## Core Features

- Secure authentication
- Document upload
- AI document summarization
- Business insights generation
- Semantic document search (RAG)
- Sales prediction using Machine Learning
- Executive dashboard
- Activity logging
